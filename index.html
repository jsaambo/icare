iCare Network International was founded by a team of young professionals driven to reduce inequalities and create opportunities for underserved communities.


Our team brings together healthcare providers, educators, mentors and advocates to deliver programs that support health, education, and personal development.


Each program is designed to empower individuals, open new opportunities, and help build stronger, more resilient communities.

Our Current Areas of Focus

Our Current Areas of Focus

Our Current Areas of Focus

Eye Health Services

Eye Health Services

We deliver community-based and school-based eye care services through free screenings, treatment including eye drops and glasses, and referrals to ensure individuals in underserved communities receive the care they need to see clearly and function well in their daily lives.

Education & Skills Development

Education & Skills Development

We support students with scholarships, school supplies, and literacy programs to help them succeed in school. Through school-based activities, we encourage children to stay engaged and build a strong foundation for their future.

Menstrual Health & Hygiene

Menstrual Health & Hygiene

We promote menstrual health education and provide menstrual hygiene products to girls and young women. These efforts help reduce stigma, support school attendance and improve overall health and confidence.

Mentorship & Career Counselling

Mentorship & Career Counselling

We connect students and young professionals with experienced leaders in clinical care, academia, research and public health. Each mentorship relationship offers career guidance, academic support and opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Our Campus Chapters

iCare Network International operates active student chapters at leading universities in Ghana. These chapters play a vital role in advancing our mission by organizing outreach programs, hosting campus events, engaging in community service and supporting national initiatives.

Through these chapters, students gain leadership experience, build professional networks and contribute directly to improving lives in underserved communities.

KNUST Chapter

KNUST Chapter

KNUST Chapter

The Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST) Chapter leads our grassroots outreach efforts, organizing community programs that deliver vision care, mentorship, and educational support in and around Kumasi. The team collaborates with local schools and community organizations to coordinate screenings, support students directly, and build practical leadership experience through service.
