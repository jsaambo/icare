Welcome to
iCare Network International

iCare Network International is a youth-led organization that serves underprivileged and remote communities in Ghana and beyond. We provide healthcare, education, mentorship, scholarships, and social support to help individuals overcome barriers and create better futures. Our work is guided by equity, compassion and a deep commitment to lasting impact.

Our Current Areas of Focus

Our Current Areas of Focus

Our Current Areas of Focus

Eye Health

Eye Health

We deliver community-based and school-based eye care services through free screenings, treatments and referrals. Individuals with limited access to vision services receive eyeglasses, medications and follow-up care to help improve their sight and overall well-being.

Education & Literacy

Education & Literacy

We support students with scholarships, books, school supplies and literacy programs. School-based activities encourage children to stay in school and build a strong foundation for their future.

Menstrual Health & Hygiene

Menstrual Health & Hygiene

We promote menstrual health education and provide menstrual hygiene products to girls and young women. These efforts help reduce stigma, support school attendance and improve overall health and confidence.

Mentorship & Career Counselling

Mentorship & Career Counselling

We connect students and young professionals with experienced leaders in clinical care, academia, research and public health. Each mentorship relationship offers career guidance, academic support and opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Our Campus Chapters

Our Campus Chapters

iCare Network International operates active student chapters at leading universities in Ghana. These chapters play a vital role in advancing our mission by organizing outreach programs, hosting campus events, engaging in community service and supporting national initiatives.

Through these chapters, students gain leadership experience, build professional networks and contribute directly to improving lives in underserved communities.

KNUST Chapter

KNUST Chapter

KNUST Chapter

The KNUST Chapter of iCare Network International is at the forefront of our grassroots efforts, organizing impactful outreach programs and community service initiatives. Driven by passionate students, this chapter plays a critical role in delivering eye health screenings, mentorship, and educational support to underserved communities in and around Kumasi.By collaborating with local schools, clinics, and organizations, the KNUST team helps bridge the gap in healthcare access and educational opportunities. Through their leadership, they’re not only transforming lives but also shaping the future of healthcare professionals and community advocates in Ghana.
